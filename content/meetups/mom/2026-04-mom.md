### 1. Session by Shrayas
**Topic:** Strongerr.in & Agentic Engineering  

- Introduced his project **Strongerr.in**, focused on gym-centric solutions.  
- Contributed **466 commits** and worked approximately **80 hours**.

#### Key Principles of Agentic Engineering:
1. Learn to steer LLMs effectively (*Start with Why?*)  
2. Set clear intentions (Sycophancy in LLMs)  
3. Move beyond web interfaces (Tool calling)  
4. Be mindful of context (LLMs have fixed limits)  
5. Plan extensively  
6. Externalize the stack  
7. Be willing to rebuild (*Code is cheap*)

### 2. Session by Rengaraj
**Topic:** The Magic of Generators  

- Explained **Python generators** and why certain code may hang.  
- Generators process data **one at a time** and give better control.

#### Key Points:
- Generator pipelines (generator → generator)  
- Compared generators vs loops:
  - Similar execution time  
  - Better **memory efficiency** and **lazy loading**  

- Suggested learning from **David Beazley**
